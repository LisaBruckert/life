Linear Fascicle Evaluation (LiFE)

Large-scale evaluation and statistical inference method for white-matter connectomes.

Copyright 2013-2014 | Franco Pestilli | pestillifranco@gmail.com

Application.

  • Evaluate the evidence supporting white-matter connectomes generated using magnetic resonance diffusion imaging and computational tractography .

  • Performe statistical inference on white-matter connectomes: Compare white-matter connectomes, show the evidence for white-matter tracts and connections between brain areas.

Dependencies.

  1. MatLab.
  2. vistasoft.
  3. Matlab Brain Anatomy (MBA).

Installation.

  1. Download LiFE.
  2. Download vistasoft.
  3. Download MBA.
  4. Start MatLab.
  5. Add LiFE and vistasoft to the matlab search path.
