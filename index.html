Linear Fascicle Evaluation (LiFE)

Large-scale evaluation method for white-matter connectomes.

LiFE is intended for:

  • Evaluating the evidence supporting white-matter connectomes generated using magnetic resonance diffusion imaging and computational tractography .

  • Performing statistical inference on white-matter connectomes: Compare white-matter connectomes, show the evidence for white-matter tracts and connections between brain areas.

LiFE is based on MatLab and requires vistasoft and Matlab Brain Anatomy (MBA) for anatomical visualization.
Installation
  1. Download LiFE.
  2. Download vistasoft.
  3. Download MBA.
  4. Start MatLab.
  5. Add LiFE and vistasoft to the matlab search path.
